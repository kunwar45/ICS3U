Unit 1: Student Orientation Course

Activity 1: Home Page versus Course Home

Answer

Which of these four pages must you always visit for every activity you do?

a. Overview
b. Learning Goals (Expectations)
c. Content
d. Assignment

Well, it's really important that you visit each page in each activity. However, the content page is your "lesson" and the assignment page contains the assignments you will be asked to complete for that activity. Skipping the content page will make it difficult for you to complete the assignment since the content page contains all of the information you will need, examples consolidating the material in the lesson for you and often hints for how to complete the assignments. Therefore, you must visit,

c. Content
d. Assignment
