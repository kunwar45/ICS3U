Assignment 1

Personalize your Profile Picture. On the MiniBar click on the menu beside your name and then click Profile. Change the boring grey outline to a picture of you, your pet, your favorite team logo, avatar or whatever you'd like. Within reason. Remember, the internet is a public place where we need to make a good impression. Only post a profile picture that you'd show your grandmother.

The maximum size of the image file you can upload is 1 mb.
